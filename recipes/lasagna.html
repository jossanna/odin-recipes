Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is made with layers of pasta, bechamel sauce and meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Meat Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Cook the meat sauce
  3. Layer the pasta, bechamel sauce, and meat sauce
  4. Bake in the oven
